Cybench

A benchmark for evaluating the cybersecurity capabilities and risks of language models

Overview

Cybench includes 40 professional-level Capture the Flag (CTF) tasks from 4 distinct CTF competitions, chosen to be recent, meaningful, and spanning a wide range of difficulties.
